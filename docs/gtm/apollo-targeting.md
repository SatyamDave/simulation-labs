# Apollo list spec — ~$49 plan, one month

**Who buys:** growth/product lead at a funded startup who already suspects the flow
leaks but has no evidence to bring to sprint planning.

## Filters

- **Titles:** Head of Growth · VP Growth · Growth Lead · Growth PM · Head of
  Product · Director of Product · Founder/CEO (only at <30-person cos)
- **Company size:** 11–200
- **Funding:** Seed, Series A, Series B (raised in last 24 months — they have
  budget and pressure)
- **Industry:** e-commerce/DTC brands, B2C SaaS, consumer fintech, marketplaces,
  health/wellness apps — anything with a self-serve signup or checkout
- **Geo:** US first (timezone + no GDPR friction on outreach)
- **Signals to prioritize** (Apollo "buying intent" / news filters, or manual):
  - launched a redesign / new onboarding / new pricing page in the last ~90 days
  - hiring for "growth" or "CRO" roles (they've admitted the problem, no one's
    in the seat yet)
  - running paid ads (Meta Ad Library check) — paying for traffic into a leaky
    funnel hurts more

## Build order

1. Export ~400 → verify emails (Apollo built-in or NeverBounce) → keep ~250 valid.
2. For each: 60 seconds on their flow → note ONE specific thing for the first
   line / touch-2 detail. No note, no send.
3. Rank: recent-redesign + running-ads companies first.

## Do NOT

- Blast unverified emails (bounces >3% poison the domain).
- Send >50/day.
- Email two people at the same company on the same day.
- Keep sending once 5 audits are booked — the phase is capped on purpose.
