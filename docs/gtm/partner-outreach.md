# Partner outreach — agencies & fractional CMOs

**Goal:** ONE partner who feeds audits. Not a partner program (phase cap: 5 audits/60 days).
**Offer:** free teardown of one of THEIR client's flows → they present it as their
discovery → client books the full audit ($1.5–3k) → partner gets 20–25% referral
fee ($300–750). Comps if asked: Retainful pays 30%, Rebuy 20%, Shopify 20%.

**Targets, in order:**
1. GetConversions (getconversions.com) — smallest, fastest yes, send first
2. KlientBoost — builds/tests landing pages per campaign; lives in the ad→page gap
3. Disruptive Advertising — has an internal CRO team = internal champion
4. (channel) Fractional CMOs via CMO Huddles / Fractional CMO FB group — join, lurk
   a week, post one useful teardown finding before any pitch

## Agency email — touch 1

**Subject:** free behavioral teardown for one of your clients

> {First line: reference specific client work or a case study of theirs.}
>
> I run swarms of browser agents — calibrated to behave like rushed, misclick-prone,
> first-time, mobile-thumb users — through one flow, and film the exact pixel where
> each segment gives up. It settles the "is it the ads or the page?" argument with
> video instead of opinions.
>
> Offer: pick one client. I'll run their checkout or signup free this week and send
> you the report — abandonment heatmap, survival curve, cursor-path video. Present
> it as your discovery. If the client wants the full audit, it's $1.5–3k and you
> take 20–25%.
>
> Sample report: {link}
>
> — Satyam, Simulation Labs
