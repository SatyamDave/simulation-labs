## Objection replies (paste-ready)

**"We use Hotjar / FullStory."** — Session replay shows where real users dropped
after they churned. We show why, before you ship: same flow, controlled segments,
reproducible run-for-run. They're complements — replay finds the fire, we find the
faulty wiring. See {landing}/#field.

**"We tried an AI audit tool."** — Was it one that clicks, or one that opines?
Most score a screenshot or write feedback. Our agents attempt the flow under
mechanical limits (click tremor, step budgets, small viewports) and fail at a
specific pixel you can watch. If your tool gave you a video of the failure, we're
the same thing — nobody else does.

**"Too expensive."** — An agency CRO audit is $8–25k and 4–6 weeks. This is $1.5–3k
in a week, and the free pass costs you a reply. If the free pass finds nothing,
you've lost nothing.

**"How is this different from QA / our E2E tests?"** — Your tests use a perfect
robot: right selector, infinite patience. Real users are rushed, imprecise, and on
phones. We test with THAT user. Passing QA and leaking 30% of checkouts are not in
tension — that gap is exactly what we measure.
